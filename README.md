# 📋 Sports Playbook Maker

A lightweight, browser-based tactical whiteboard for designing sports plays. This tool allows coaches and players to visualize strategies for **Football, Basketball, Baseball, and Water Polo** using a simple drag-and-drop interface.

## 🚀 Features

* **Multi-Sport Support:** Toggle between specialized field/court designs for 4 different sports.
* **Arrow Tool:** Click and drag to draw directional movement or passing lanes.
* **Player Management:** Add "O" (Offense) and "X" (Defense) dots to the field.
* **Move & Erase:** Reposition players on the fly or remove specific elements.
* **Text Tool:** Label positions or add tactical notes (e.g., "Screen here").
* **Export to PNG:** Save your finished play as a high-quality image file.

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. **Select Sport:** Use the dropdown menu to choose your field.
2. **Add Players:** Click the blue **+ O** or orange **+ X** buttons to drop players onto the center of the field.
3. **Position Players:** Select the **Move** tool and drag the dots to their starting positions.
4. **Draw Plays:** Use the **Arrow** tool to click and drag movement paths.
5. **Add Labels:** Use the **Text** tool to click on the field and type player names or instructions.
6. **Save:** Click **Save PNG** to download the diagram to your computer.
